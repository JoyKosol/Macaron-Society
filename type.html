Classic French

These are the quintessential macarons, known for their delicate almond meringue shells that sandwich flavorful fillings. The shells are light, airy, and slightly crispy on the outside, with a soft, chewy interior. Classic flavors include vanilla, chocolate, coffee, pistachio, raspberry, lemon, and more.

Chocolate

These macarons feature cocoa-infused shells or shells made with melted chocolate, resulting in a rich, decadent flavor. They are often filled with chocolate ganache or other chocolate-based fillings, offering a delightful treat for chocolate lovers.

Fruit Flavored

These macarons are infused with vibrant fruit flavors, adding a refreshing and tangy twist to the traditional almond base. Popular fruit flavors include strawberry, mango, passionfruit, orange, and blueberry, among others, creating a burst of fruity goodness in every bite.

Nut Flavored

In addition to almonds, macarons can also be made with other nuts like pistachios, hazelnuts, and walnuts. These nut-flavored macarons bring a unique and nutty taste profile to the table, offering a delightful alternative for those seeking different flavor experiences.

Seasonal

Some bakeries and patisseries offer seasonal macarons that celebrate the flavors of each season. For example, you might find pumpkin spice macarons for fall, peppermint macarons for winter, and tropical-inspired macarons for summer, adding a touch of seasonal flair to these delightful treats.

Exotic

Macarons also come in a variety of exotic flavors that push the boundaries of creativity. Examples include matcha green tea, earl grey tea, lavender, rose, salted caramel, lychee, and more, offering a fusion of unique and adventurous tastes.
